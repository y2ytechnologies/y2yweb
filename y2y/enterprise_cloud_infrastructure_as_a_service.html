Enterprise Cloud – Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)

 



Enterprise IT is transforming from Traditional IT to Cloud Computing. Cloud is changing from IT as a function to IT as a Service to business. y TO Y Technologies helps you to bridge the knowledge gap and consult in transforming the enterprise Traditional IT to Cloud Computing.


Enterprise Cloud – Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)

course is a 4 days session emphasis on how to transform Traditional IT Infrastructure to Enterprise Cloud – Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS).
   

Target Audience

If you are an IT Infrastructure Architects, Designers, Engineers, Administrators, Analyst, and Consultants who aspire to transform their skills to Cloud IaaS or your organization is in transition to move their existing IT infrastructure to cloud IaaS or want to setup a green field environment in cloud IaaS. Service providers who wanted to setup a Public Cloud. This course will help you Strategize, Plan, Architect, Design, Build, and Run & Secure Enterprise Cloud - Infrastructure as a Service
   
Prerequisite Candidate should have basic understanding of Enterprise IT Infrastructure
   
Facilitator

Y TO Y’s Consultants are having average of decade experience in IT Infrastructure Consulting, Architecting, Design and implementation for Fortune 100 Companies.
